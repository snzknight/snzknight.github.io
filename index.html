Short Bio

I am currently a third-year master's student at Ocean University of China, supervised by Prof. Haiyong Zheng. I received my bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering from Qingdao University of Technology in 2023. My research interests lie in deep learning and computer vision, focusing on developing advanced techniques and applications in these fields.

My recent work concentrates on interactive applications of AI large models, advocating for natural and efficient human-computer interaction to enhance the perceptual and responsive capabilities of large models, thereby promoting their seamless integration into practical scenarios.
